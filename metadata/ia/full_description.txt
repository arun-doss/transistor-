<p><i>Transistor</i> es un application de radio con un minimalistic approach, that may not be to everyone's liking. It has no radio station discovery feature. <i>Transistor</i> only offers a very simple search option and it imports audio streaming links, when you tap on them in un navigator web</p><p><br><b>FOLIO A QUESTIONES</b></p><dl><dt>COMO IO POTE ADDER UN STATION DE RADIO?</dt><dd>There are three ways pro adder un station de radio a Transistor: Use Search, add playlist file address (M3U, PLS), enter a raw stream address.</dd><dt>WHERE DO THE RADIO STATION SEARCH RESULTS COME FROM?</dt><dd>Transistor recerca le base de datos in linea de radio-browser.info. Tu pote adjutar al communitate de radio-browser.info addinte le station de radio mancante (http://www.radio-browser.info/gui/#!/add) a lor base de datos.</dd><dt>HOW CAN I CURATE THE RADIO STATION LIST?</dt><dd>Usa le gesto de glissamento pro deler un station de radio del lista. Further optiones de modification pote esser activate in Parametros. If enabled, tap and hold a radio station to edit its name, image or streaming link.</dd></dl>
